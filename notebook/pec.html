Bonus proposal

  • Define three levels
  • Level_0 - less than 3 --- no bonus or restricted
  • Level_1 - more than 3 and less than the mean plus one standard deviation --- 100% of bonus
  • Level_2 - more than the mean plus one standard deviation - more than 100% of bonus

Tests

Traditionally, in statistics, you need a p-value of less than 0.05 to reject the null hypothesis. In this case, the null hypothesis was normality. Because our p value is greater than 0.05 (actually, it's greater than 0.10), we cannot reject the null hypothesis. Therefore, we have not proven that this data set is different from normality.

buildname = 'aer_pc_meet_hugo'
def list2slide( pics, outfile ):
    from pptx import Presentation
    from pptx.util import Inches, Pt
    """ takes a list of pictures and creates a slideshow in powerpoint
        pics    = list of pics
        outfile = filename.pptx
    """
    pres = Presentation()
    for pic in pics:
        slide = pres.slides.add_slide( pres.slide_layouts[1] )
        slide.shapes.add_picture( pic, Inches(0.6), Inches(2))
    pres.save(outfile)
    del(pres)
    
a = !ls *.png
filename = buildname+'.pptx'
list2slide( a, filename)
